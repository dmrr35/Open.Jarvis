# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want J.A.R.V.I.S to be a welcoming, useful, and safe project for learners, builders, and contributors. We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when something lands poorly.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and helping users.
- Be careful with security-sensitive topics such as credentials, plugins, desktop automation, and logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing real API keys, tokens, logs, or screenshots that expose sensitive data.
- Encouraging unsafe use of desktop automation, plugins, or system commands.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the repository maintainers or the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md` when the concern involves sensitive information.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable steps to protect the community and project.
